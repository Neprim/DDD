### Deep Dark Dungeon by Neprim
---
#### Just a simple rogue-like in Bash
---

#### Controls: 

##### Dungeon Map: 
    wasd - moving 
    i - open/close inventory 
    l - look around 
    z - abort action 
    > - descend down 
    < - ascend up 
    , - pick up items 
    Space - skip turn 

#### Inventory: 
    [a-z] - choose an item 
    E - switch inventory/equipment
